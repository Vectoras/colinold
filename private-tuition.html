Why Me

Over the past 32 years, I have privately tutored all manner of students, including both school pupils and adults. I had only ever anticipated tutoring children of school age for SATs exams (11 and 14-year-olds), GCSEs (16-year- olds) and entrance exams for private schools. However, after tutoring a man in the navy who needed an English qualification, and coaching a Spanish final year student for her re-takes, I decided to make myself available for adults as well.

I am happy to travel to local destinations and my hourly fee is very reasonable when compared to what other tutors or tuition centres are charging. Tutoring in a more relaxed environment, in familiar surroundings over a cup of coffee, is often more preferable to the usual more formal, impersonal setting of a school or college.

Step 1

Have a good look at the info on this website to determine whether you feel that you could benefit from my teaching experience to secure your success.

Step 2

Contact me with any questions you still want answered. I am happy to be contacted here, by phone, e-mail or Facebook – whichever is easiest!

Step 3

I would then arrange an initial meeting gratis, to establish your specific requirements and work alongside you to organise a programme of study. At the same time, I would explain the hourly and half-hourly fee which is determined by the examination level being studied.

Step 4

We would set aside a regular lesson, usually once a week, to focus on the material to be studied and look forward to a successful outcome!
